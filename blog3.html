How to create patient-friendly hospital using NABH guidelines?

Putting "Patient First" through quality and design excellence.

September 26, 2024 | By Nayesha Health Care

Creating a patient-friendly hospital is about more than aesthetics; it is a commitment to "Quality Care With Personal Touch". By aligning with NABH guidelines, hospitals can transform their service delivery and patient outcomes.

1. Smart Design & Planning

A patient-friendly environment starts with the physical layout. NABH standards emphasize safety and ease of navigation.

Architectural Expertise

Utilizing a skilled architectural and technical team ensures the hospital meets NABH standards from start to finish.

Modern Automation

Blending experience with state-of-the-art automation technology gives hospitals a competitive edge in patient care.

2. Implementing Quality Tools

To ensure consistent care, hospitals must implement recognized quality tools within their healthcare sector.

  • TQM & 5S: Improving total quality management and workplace organization.
  • 6-Sigma: Reducing errors in healthcare processes to ensure "Patient First" outcomes.
  • ISO Standards: Maintaining global quality benchmarks for medical services.

3. Staff Training & Excellence

A hospital is only as friendly as its staff. Training is crucial for delivering exceptional patient care.

Nayesha Healthcare’s Training Service Excellence Module focuses on teaching staff how to transform the healthcare experience today.
