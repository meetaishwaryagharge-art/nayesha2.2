Sadguru Moredada Multispecialty Hospital

Planning a Landmark 550-Bedded Tertiary Care Institution

About The Project

Sadguru Moredada Multispecialty Hospital is a visionary initiative by the Akhil Bhartiya Shree Swami Samarth Gurupeeth, aimed at establishing a 550-bedded tertiary care hospital in Trimbakeshwar, Nashik. This ambitious project is designed to bridge the critical gap in access to advanced healthcare services for the residents of Trimbakeshwar and nearby rural areas of Nashik district.

The hospital is being developed on a 21-acre land parcel allocated by the trust, with a clear objective of creating a state-of-the-art healthcare institution equipped with advanced diagnostic and clinical facilities. The vision is to provide high-quality, comprehensive medical care to underserved populations while maintaining strong standards of efficiency, safety, and patient-centric services.

Nayesha Healthcare has been associated with this project as a medical planning consultant from its inception, playing a crucial role in shaping the hospital’s clinical and functional framework. From the early planning stages, Nayesha has been actively involved in developing a structured approach to departmental planning, service mix optimization, and patient flow design, ensuring alignment with tertiary care requirements.

The engagement extends across multiple phases of the project, including detailed medical planning, infrastructure alignment, and coordination with design and execution teams. The focus has been on creating a hospital that is not only large in scale but also efficient, scalable, and operationally sustainable.

Given the scale and complexity of the project, special attention has been paid to zoning, inter-departmental connectivity, and future expansion capabilities, ensuring that the hospital can adapt to evolving healthcare needs over time.

Through its continuous involvement, Nayesha Healthcare is contributing to the development of a landmark healthcare institution that will significantly enhance access to tertiary care services in the region.

  • Strategic Location Planning in Trimbakeshwar, Nashik.
  • Advanced Modular Operation Theaters and ICU setups.
