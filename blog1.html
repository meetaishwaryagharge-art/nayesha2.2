Are you preparing for NABH Accreditation the wrong way?

Ensuring your hospital standards meet global guidelines effectively.

September 26, 2024 | By Nayesha Health Care

Getting your hospital accredited is often seen as a stressful task, but it is an essential step toward providing invaluable service to society.

Many healthcare facilities approach NABH accreditation as a mere paperwork exercise. However, the true intent of these guidelines is to put the "Patient First"[cite: 37]. If your preparation doesn't focus on quality tools like TQM, 5S, and 6-Sigma, you might be missing the point.

"Accreditation is not just about the certificate; it's about shifting the culture to deliver cutting-edge healthcare management solutions".

Common Pitfalls in Preparation

Technical Neglect

Ignoring the integration of architectural and technical standards during the planning phase.

Staff Training

Failing to train staff through specialized modules like the Service Excellence Module.
